Meet the Traders

Not all pins are on boards. Learn to spot the Cast Members who carry the hidden gems.



Spot the Gear

Cast Members carry pins in two main ways. Look for these visuals:

The Neck Lanyard

Most Common

Who wears it: Merchandise hosts and Greeters.

Pro Tip: Look for the "reverse" pin. If a pin is turned backward, it's a mystery trade or Trivia!

The Hip Pack (Pouch)

Gold Mine

Who wears it: Custodial, Managers, and Guest Relations.

Pro Tip: Since these pins are hidden at waist level, they get traded less oftenâ€”meaning better pins for you!

Best Roles to Find

Custodial Staff

Why: They roam freely and often carry the best pins because people forget to check their hip pouches.

Merchandise Hosts

Why: The easiest to find. They usually stand at the front of stores greeting guests.

Hotel Concierge

Why: If you are visiting a resort, the front desk almost always has a "Pin Book" or board behind the counter.
